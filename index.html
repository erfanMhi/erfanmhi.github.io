Erfan (Er+Fun) Miahi

For over 7 years, I have been actively involved in conducting machine learning research. Recently, my focus has been on contributing to the development of fundamental reinforcement learning research in collaboration with students and scientists from DeepMind and UoA. Driven by my passion for creating exceptional products, I have started to apply my expertise in reinforcement learning to tackle real-world challenges. This began with optimizing the chemical levels in a water treatment plant and has since expanded to the realm of algorithmic trading.
